Dr. Yin Shi Hoo

DDS (Gold Medalist) UKM,
MFD RCS (Edin),
Fellow of the International College of Continuing Dental Education (ICCDE),
Certified Invisalign Provider,
Registered with Malaysian & Australian Dental Council,
Certificate in World Clinical Laser Institute,
Member of ITI,
Member of Malaysian Dental Association

Academic and Professional Achievements:

Dr. Hoo was the recipient of the Honor Scholarship Award in 2004-2005, which was awarded to the top achiever of the faculty. She graduated from the National University of Malaysia in 2009 with a Gold Medal Award (Best All-Round Student) under full scholarship sponsorship. ​

Besides being a firm believer in evidence-based dentistry, Dr. Hoo is a keen researcher. She has received a Gold Medal for winning SEAADE-GC Dental Asia Student Prevention Table Clinician Competition in year 2009. She was also awarded the Best Presenter Award in oral presentation at CDA/MDA Joint International Scientific Convention (2012).

​

After graduation, Dr. Hoo was appointed as one of the Dental Officers in the Department of Oral Maxillofacial Surgery and Paediatric Dentistry by the Ministry of Health, Malaysia. Besides her hospital-based experience, she has worked alongside other specialists which gave her many opportunities to practice all specialties and subspecialties of dentistry as well as to serve people of diverse cultures.
