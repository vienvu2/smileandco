ABOUT US

Dentistry meets Art at

Smile & Co.

Dentistry and Art are often regarded as distinct; either a person can't be serious about both or an interest in one must supersede the other. In reality, both are distinct but also one in the same.

Founded by Dr. Hoo, who is considered by many as one of the gifted dentists of her generation, Smile & Co. has been shaped around the vision of bringing beauty to dentistry.

At Smile & Co., a closely curated team has been selected for their technical skill and artistic sensibility, to provide clients with smiles that blur the imaginary line between Dentistry and Art.

Drop by for a consultation with one of our passionate dental practitioners, as an attractive smile is your key to a million possibilities.
